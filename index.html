About BZ


Marc Altimari and Connor Morency are Burlington Zephyr. This project is an outlet for their love of music and desire for creative expression. Marc is the wizard behind the curtain of intricate sounds while Connor adds an angsty flair. Both sing and contribute to the songwriting.

Marc and Connor first became friends when they went to a haunted house without their parents in sixth grade. Since then they have collaborated on a range of creative projects, many of which were through their high school in the suburbs of Chicago (like this one, for example). Music is what they love the most.

Marc is currently a resident surgeon at RUSH University Medical Center in Chicago; Connor is currently pursuing his PhD in aerospace engineering at the University of Colorado, Boulder. While their scientific careers take priority, they are devout musicians who are serious about their craft. "Summer Always Ends" is just the beginning.
