Marc Altimari and Connor Morency are Burlington Zephyr. This project is an outlet for our love of music and desire for creative expression. Marc is the wizard behind the curtain of intricate sounds, while Connor adds an angsty flair. Both sing and contribute to the songwriting.

Marc and Connor first became friends when they went to a haunted house without their parents in sixth grade. Since then they have collaborated on a range of creative projects, many of which were through their high school in the suburbs of Chicago. Music is what they love the most.
